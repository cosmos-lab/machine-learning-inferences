GST Registration – Mandatory Conditions
GST registration is mandatory if aggregate annual turnover exceeds ₹40 lakh for goods suppliers.
GST registration is mandatory if aggregate annual turnover exceeds ₹20 lakh for service providers.
GST registration is mandatory for inter-state suppliers.
GST registration is mandatory for e-commerce sellers and operators.
GST registration is mandatory for casual taxable persons.
GST registration is mandatory for non-resident taxable persons.
GST registration is mandatory for persons liable to pay tax under reverse charge.

GST Registration – Online Application
GST registration can be applied online through the GST portal.
The official GST registration website is www.gst.gov.in
.

GST Registration – Documents Required
PAN card of the applicant is required for GST registration.
Aadhaar card of the applicant is required for GST registration.
Proof of business address is required for GST registration.
Bank account details are required for GST registration.
Photograph of the applicant is required for GST registration.

GST Registration – Proof of Business Address
Proof of business address can be an electricity bill.
Proof of business address can be a rent agreement.
Proof of business address can be a property tax receipt.
Rent agreement is required if the business premises are rented.
Ownership proof is required if the business premises are owned.

GST Registration – Entity-wise Requirements
GST registration for a proprietorship requires PAN and Aadhaar of the proprietor.
GST registration for a partnership requires PAN of the firm and PAN of the partners.
GST registration for a company requires certificate of incorporation.

GST Registration – Process and Timeline
GST registration process includes submission of application.
GST registration process includes verification of application.
GST registration usually takes 3 to 7 working days after successful verification.
GST number is issued after approval of GST application.

GST Registration – Fees and GSTIN
GST registration is free of cost on the GST portal.
GST number is also known as GSTIN.
GSTIN is a 15-digit unique identification number.
